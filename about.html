I was born on October 28 1999

Fourth child to Dr Tali Vishne and Professor Uzi Vishne, younger sibling to Gal, Ariel and Hadar.

As a young child we lived part time in the US, when my father worked at Yale and later at Princeton, where I learned to speak English.

Growing up, we had many pets. Mostly cats.

I studied at Moreshet Zvulun in elementary school and Tzfira in high school. Then I did national service for 2 years, 1 at The Family Affairs Aid Unit Under The Courts and 1 at The Veterinary Hospital in Beit Dagan. Right after that I started studying Chemistry and Biology in Bar Ilan university. after one year I transferred my studies to Computer Science and Biology. That didn't go so well so I transferred to John Bryce's Full Stack Web and Full Stack Python.

While studying in university I also did a course on dog training and have a certificate for dog training. I also participated for a couple of years in Bar Ilan's begginer's choir, did some professional vocal training and recorded 4 songs professionally (covers of musical theatre songs).

This project is meant to show the user about various popular virtual coins.
